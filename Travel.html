About:

During the last six years my family and i spend two weeks every year in an area in spian called cambrils next to the popular tourist spot of Salou.

Cambrils is one of the most popular places to head for a picturesque getaway on the Costa Dorada. It sits about 100 km south of Barcelona on Spain's craggy and beautiful North-East coastline. A classy fishing town, it is popular amongst Spaniards and Europeans alike. They come for its boundless sunshine, its world-class gastronomy and its central location. Cambrils acts as the more peaceful and grown-up alternative to neighbouring Salou. It is the perfect place to bring the whole family - the kids'll love its close proximity to Port Aventura theme park and its endless beaches, the teenagers'll be dying to make the most of the nightlife in Salou and the grownups can enjoy the culture of the old town and the quality restaurants. Cambrils' location is another of its pulls.Sitting in the heart of the Costa Daurada it provides the perfect base for exploring the area. Hire a car and discover the local wine region. Take a train along the cove-filled coastline. Or enjoy the golf, theme parks and water parks at the massive Port Aventura resort. There's something for everybody in Cambrils.
